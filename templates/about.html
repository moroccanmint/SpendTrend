About Our App

The application aims to predict the estimated average grocery expenditure. This applies the concept of data analysis and utilizes the statistical method called Linear regression. This technique assumes whether there is a linear relationship between the one or more independent variable and the dependent variable. It is a great tool for modeling the relationship between variables. It's a simple but effective way to understand these relationships.


Users have the flexibility to choose between one and three independent variables, which could include total income, the number of household members, and the age of the primary shopper. These factors are crucial in determining the dependent variable, total grocery expenditure.


To make accurate predictions, users need to input data for each variables for both dependent and independent variables covering both past and expected future values. This ensures the model can make more reliable forecasts, optimizing its performance.


Overall, the developers are confident that this Grocery Expenditure Prediction application can significantly benefit consumers by helping them manage their finances better. Additionally, it has the potential to enhance the overall grocery shopping experience for everyone involved.
